Template Section: Code: Loops

Templates have no notion of loops. However, by using a combination of the !!Add (or !!Sub), !!Goto, and !!Label code commands, loops may be simulated.

!!Set number 10
!!///////////////////////////////////////////////
!!Label loopTop
Hello, Nurse!  @@number@@
!!Sub number 1
!!If @@number@@ > 0
!!Goto loopTop
!!Endif
