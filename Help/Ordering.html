Ordering

How do I order?

Workspace Whiz adopts a WinZip-style shareware approach. You are given 21 days to evaluate Workspace Whiz!, and then you must order it.

Ordering is a simple process. The ordering system is provided by the very popular and reliable Register Now! service.

Workspace Whiz costs $10.00 U.S. Please read the license agreement first.

Please go to the Workspace Whiz! Ordering site for more information.

Why shareware?

Several years ago, I began creating an add-in by the name of Project File Open. I built it entirely for myself as a way to speed-up some common operations I performed daily under Developer Studio. One day, a coworker saw it and demanded he be able to use it, too. Soon, everyone in the office was using my add-in, and the feature requests began flying in. I renamed the add-in to the Workspace Utilities.

Quite a while later, that same coworker wondered what I thought about posting the Workspace Utilities to CodeGuru. I did, and more user feedback than I could ever imagine began pouring in. Thousands of downloads later, I began creating Workspace Whiz This time, it wasn't about me, but it was about all my users.

Workspace Whiz has been in active (i.e. spare time) development now for well over a year. I have tried to add every feature my users have ever requested. I've succeeded in implementing most features.

In turning Workspace Whiz into a high quality product, it has cost me both time and money. To justify some of the expense, I ask that users who find Workspace Whiz useful contribute to Workspace Whiz's future development by ordering it.
